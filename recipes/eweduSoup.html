Ingredients


  • Water
  • Salt,pepper, ewedu leaves
  • Maggi seasoning, Onions, garlic and ginger

Steps


  1. Put water(2 litres) on fire
  2. Slice the Onions garlic and ginger
  3. Add maggi and other seasoning
  4. Leave to boil for 15 minutes
  5. Add the plaintain and the goatmeat
  6. Leave for 15/minutes
  7. Stir and and serve hot!!!
